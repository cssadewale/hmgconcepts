Our Origin

Where It All Started

HMG Concepts — full name, His Marvellous Grace Educational Consult — was founded in 2015 by Adewale Samson Adeagbo, a secondary school teacher with a burning question: why were Nigerian students being failed by a system that could be fixed?

The answer wasn't to complain. It was to build. So that's what he did.

"I always wanted to solve classroom problems with technology. That drive is what led me to study Computer Science Education — and it's what still drives every decision we make at HMG Concepts today."

— Adewale Samson Adeagbo, Founder

What began as a one-man tutoring operation has grown into a full brand with three subsidiaries: HMG Academy (virtual schooling), HMG Technologies (EdTech & data solutions), and HMG Media (content & digital presence). Through it all, the philosophy has never changed: Learning Deliberately. Teaching Authentically.

Today HMG Concepts serves students across Lagos and Ogun State — and increasingly, anywhere in Nigeria with an internet connection. Our teachers are seasoned professionals. Our tools are built on real data. Our content is grounded in lived classroom experience.

The Journey

From Blackboard to Data Science

Adewale's teaching career began in 2009. For 15+ years, he has taught Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry across nursery, primary, and secondary schools in Lagos and Ogun State. That classroom time wasn't just teaching — it was research. He was watching, every day, what worked and what didn't.

The turning point came in late 2024 when he joined the Federal Government's 3MTT programme. Something that had always been a conviction — that data and technology could solve classroom problems — finally found its technical language. He went deep: machine learning, Python, data analysis, model deployment.

Today he has 12 deployed ML and EdTech projects on GitHub, is actively enrolled in three advanced upskilling programmes, and serves as a SkillBuild Hub Ambassador. His data science work — covering insurance fraud detection, delivery delay prediction, student risk profiling, and more — is built on the same clarity he developed teaching quadratic equations to teenagers.

"Data science taught me that if you can think through a problem clearly, you can find a path to a solution — even in unfamiliar territory."

— Adewale Samson Adeagbo

He continues to teach at God of Seed Academy and tutors across multiple platforms. Not because he has to — but because the classroom is where everything he knows was tested and proven.
